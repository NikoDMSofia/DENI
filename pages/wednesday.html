It's Wednesday, Audrey.

Today is about mobility.

Before every workout, it is necessary to warming up the body. This will help prevent injuries. Also after we finish the full day's workout, we will stretch well to prevent cramps and help the muscles shape up properly. I have selected a few exercises that we can start with. Don't rush. Take all the time you need to do the exercise correctly. Today we are not chasing a number, but rather the correct execution of the exercises. Every day before work out we are warming the body, let's dive in

Warming-up
before every workout
We warm up the body from top to bottom. Starting from the neck, shoulders, arms (elbows, wrists), back, hips, legs (knees, ankles)
1st exercise
Here it is not important how you stand up but how you squat, that is, squat as slowly as possible so that there is a negative load on the thigh and it works for strength and endurance, if you have any severe pain in your knees, stop here, you will do 4 sets of as many repetitions as you can do based on your own feeling, not exceeding 10
2nd exercise
Sitting on a chair with your back straight and your knee slightly raised, extend and hold the leg in question forward, doing it slowly and without touching the foot to the ground during the exercise 4x10 on each leg
3th exercises
The important thing here is for the supporting leg to be slightly bent and the back straight, lower as far as possible without bending the knee m ore than indicated and stand up 4x10 on each side
4th exercises
the important thing here is that the support is on the heels and the feet should be shoulder width apart 3x12
5th exercises
Lean forward towards the wall until you feel a slight stretch in your calf, then start lifting yourself up on your toes as much as possible 4x10-15
