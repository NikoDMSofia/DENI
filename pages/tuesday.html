Before every workout, it is necessary to warming up the body. This will help prevent injuries. Also after we finish the full day's workout, we will stretch well to prevent cramps and help the muscles shape up properly.
I have selected a few exercises that we can start with.
Don't rush. Take all the time you need to do the exercise correctly. Today we are not chasing a number, but rather the correct execution of the exercises.

1st
1. The width of the arms should be so large that when bent, the elbows reach a right angle 3 times as much as you can.
2nd

3rd
3. Lie on your stomach, raise your torso, elbows and wrists as high as possible, hold for a second and smoothly return to the starting position 3x10-12
4th
4. Grab the elastic band almost at the ends and with your arms outstretched and your wrists straight, stretch it as much as possible and hold for 3x12
