warming up

We warm up the body from top to bottom. Starting from the neck, shoulders, arms (elbows, wrists), back, hips, legs (knees, ankles)   

Main exercises

exercise - 1

The width of the arms should be so large that when bent, the elbows reach a right angle 3 times as much as you can.
   

-

exercise - 2

With your back straight but not leaning, explode the dumbbells above your head, bending your elbows to form a right angle 3x10-12 with 1 or 2 kg   

-

exercise - 3

Lie on your stomach, raise your torso, elbows, and wrists as high as possible, hold for a second, and smoothly return to the starting position 3x10-12    

-

exercise - 4

You grab the elastic band almost at the ends and with your arms outstretched and your wrists straight, stretch it as much as possible and hold for 3x12    

-

exercise - 5

You hold your wrists without a bend in them and perform the movement smoothly 3x10 12 with 1/2 kg in your hand  

-

exercise - 6

Back straight, knees slightly bent and elbows high without moving, only 3x10 12 with 1/2kg
