Warming-up

We warm up the body from top to bottom. Starting from the neck, shoulders, arms (elbows, wrists), back, hips, legs (knees, ankles)

1st Exercise

Here it's not important how you stand up but how you squat, that is, squat as slowly as possible so that there is a negative load on the thigh and it works for strength and endurance, if you have any severe pain in your knees, stop here, you will do 4 sets of as many repetitions as you can do based on your own feeling, not exceeding 10

2nd Exercise

Sitting on a chair with your back straight and your knee slightly raised, extend and hold the leg in question forward, doing it slowly and without touching the foot to the ground during the exercise 4x10 on each leg

3th Exercise

The important thing here is for the supporting leg to be slightly bent and the back straight, lower as far as possible without bending the knee more than indicated and stand up 4x10 on each side
